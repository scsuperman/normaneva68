About Run and Shoot: GOAL!

Run without hitting obstacles and opposing team players. collect as much blue power as you can and hit the ball harder. Then direct the ball and try to pass the goalkeeper. You can score your goal and move on to the next level! According to the power you collect, the height of the ball increases and the probability of the goalkeeper to save decreases. If you want, you can transfer between halftime and change your character. hitting sticks loses 1 power. hitting obstacles lose 10 power

How to Play

Player movement - Left arrow key for move left - Right arrow key for move right - Spacebar for Jump Ball movement - Left and Right arrow keys - If you can score a goal you pass the level
