About Swipe and Clear

Swipe and Clear is a challenging and addictive puzzle game that puts your strategic skills to the test! In each level, you’ll face a grid filled with scattered colorful blocks. Your goal is simple: drag and move the blocks to form matches of 3 or more of the same color, making them disappear. But beware! If a single block of any color is left behind, the level will restart. Plan every move carefully to clear all the blocks and progress through 20 uniquely designed levels. With intuitive mechanics and engaging puzzles, Swipe and Clear will keep you thinking and entertained for hours!

How to Play

Drag and move the blocks across the grid to create matches of 3 or more of the same color
